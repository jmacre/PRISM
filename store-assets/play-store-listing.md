## Full description (max 4000 chars)
```
PRISM is a fast, satisfying match-3 puzzle wrapped in a neon-purple aesthetic.

Match three or more gems of the same color to clear them. Build cascades by
matching gems that drop into new positions. Trigger powerups by matching 4+ in
a row, and watch your score and streak multipliers climb.

— GAMEPLAY —
• Match 3 to clear, 4 for ZAP (row + column), 5 for BOMB (5×5 blast),
  6 for INFERNO (board burn), 7+ for VORTEX (board destroyed).
• Catch the rhythm — chain 5 cascades for FEVER MODE: 3× score and the
  timer freezes while you rip through combos.
• Multipliers (×2, ×5, ×10), shuffle gems, and chained "locked" cells
  to break every run feels different.

— PRISMS —
The game's signature gem. Swap a prism into any colored gem and the whole
board's matching color clears. Stack two prisms together for a full-board
wipe and a massive score bonus.

— PROGRESSION —
The timer ceiling tightens as you play, demanding faster decisions.
Milestones top up the timer at score thresholds, and your best score is
saved across runs.

— FEATURES —
• Touch-first design with adaptive touch zones
• Edge-to-edge full-screen immersive mode
• Per-powerup haptic feedback (toggleable)
• Original WAV soundtrack + responsive sound design
• Single-tap pause / Android back button support
• Built for Android 15 (API 35) with 16 KB page size support

No ads. No microtransactions. Just gems, cascades, and the chase for a
better score.
```
