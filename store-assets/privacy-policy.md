# PRISM — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-22_

PRISM ("the app") is developed and published by JM Games. This document
explains what data the app collects, how it's used, and what choices you have.

## TL;DR
- The app collects nothing.
- No personal information leaves your device.
- No third-party SDKs, no analytics, no ads.

## Data we collect
**None.** PRISM is a single-player offline puzzle game.

The only data the app stores is your local game state:
- Best score
- Current run state (board, score, timer)
- Audio / haptic preferences (mute toggles)

This data is saved exclusively on your device using browser localStorage
(via the Capacitor WebView). It never leaves your device. We do not have
servers that can access it.

## Data we do not collect
- Your name, email, phone number, or any account info
- Your device's IDFA / GAID / advertising ID
- Your location
- Your contacts, photos, or any other personal content
- Crash reports, analytics, or telemetry of any kind
- Anything else

## Network requests
The app may load static assets bundled with the app (game audio, images).
It does not make any network requests to remote servers during gameplay.

## In-app purchases / ads
There are no in-app purchases.
There are no advertisements.

## Third-party services
None. PRISM uses only:
- The Capacitor framework (offline, runs the WebView)
- Standard Android system APIs (vibration, audio, display)

No third-party SDK is included that collects, transmits, or processes
user data.

## Children's privacy
The app is suitable for general audiences. Because we collect zero
data, we do not knowingly or unknowingly collect data from children
under 13.

## Changes to this policy
If anything about data collection changes (e.g., we add an opt-in
analytics SDK in a future version), this document will be updated and
the in-app About screen will reflect the new policy.
